Leonardo Chaves Borges Cardoso

Associate Professor · Universidade Federal de Viçosa  |  Visiting · Texas A&M University

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Agricultural Economics at Universidade Federal de Viçosa, Brazil, and currently a Visiting Researcher at Texas A&M University.

My research sits at the intersection of applied economics, agricultural policy, and public health. I use quasi-experimental methods (DiD, RDD, shift-share) with Brazilian administrative microdata to study how agricultural and environmental shocks affect health outcomes, labor markets, and economic inequality.
